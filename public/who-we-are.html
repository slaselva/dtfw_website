we are

We are the workers, the makers, the artists. We are the ones who get things done, no matter what. We thrive in bringing your vision to life.

history

Downtown FabWorks was founded in the Fall of 2019 by Daniel Krall, with a mission to bring some of the most talented people from various trades under one roof. Combining his nearly 20 years of experience in the Exhibit, Broadcast Television, Construction, and Events industries, he wanted to create a company that could provide solutions for a variety of customers, while forming a team of people with diverse skills and interests that are adept at problem solving but with a knack for thinking outside the box.

doing good work

  • With over a century of combined experience in multiple environments, we are your solution to fabricate your vision.
  • That vision is a reflection of you, and your company. We see it as our primary goal to ensure that that vision comes to light.
  • We take a collaborative approach with every client and every project. This means you have a partner in Downtown Fabworks, not just a vendor. Our success is defined by the success of your project.

architectural

Our team includes the technical design and project management that you can trust to ensure that the finishes in your environment happen on time, on budget, and just as you had envisioned.

events

We live for the deadlines and thrive in the chaos of being part of large events. From conventions to festivals, arenas to domes, our team has what it takes to fabricate your scenery, big and small.

experiences

Brand activations, themed environments and broadcast television. This is where we feel we excel. Be creative, have fun and make your vision unique to you. We'll make it happen.
